FAQS

Questions, Answered.

How is Whyzer different from ChatGPT or Perplexity?
ChatGPT and Perplexity are general-purpose AI tools. Whyzer is purpose-built for enterprise sellers — it has deep integrations with SEC EDGAR, earnings call databases, and financial data sources. It doesn't just retrieve information; it synthesizes financial signals into boardroom-ready Points of View that map directly to executive priorities. The output is deal-ready, not research-ready.
How does Whyzer work?
Enter any company name. Whyzer retrieves their latest SEC filings (10-K, 10-Q, 8-K), earnings call transcripts, investor presentations, and board-level documents. Our AI runs 847 specialized prompts across this data to extract KPIs, executive priorities, and material risks. Within minutes, you receive 3–5 executive-ready Points of View tied to specific financial signals.
Is my data secure?
Yes. Whyzer is SOC 2 Type II compliant. Your account research and sales intelligence are never used to train AI models, never shared with third parties, and never visible to other users. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Enterprise plans include SSO, role-based access controls, and compliance documentation.
Does Whyzer cover international companies?
Yes. Whyzer covers public companies globally — including those listed on NYSE, NASDAQ, LSE, TSX, ASX, and major European and Asian exchanges. For private companies, we aggregate available intelligence from press releases, news, LinkedIn data, and investor documents. Coverage improves continuously as we expand our data partnerships.
Can Whyzer integrate with my CRM?
CRM integration (Salesforce, HubSpot, and others) is available on Corporate plans. This includes bi-directional sync, account enrichment directly into your CRM fields, Slack real-time alerts, and custom workflow triggers. Elite users can export research in CRM-ready formats manually.
What's included in The Vault?
The Vault is Jamal Reimer's complete enterprise sales playbook — built from closing $160M+ in SaaS deals. It includes: The Pipeline Flywheel, Elite Sellers Playbook, MDA Masterclass, Executive Outreach Course, Top/Middle/Bottom Funnel Playbooks, and access to live monthly sessions with Jamal. Exclusive to Elite and Corporate subscribers.
Why is there a free tier?
We believe the best way to prove our value is to let you experience it. The free tier gives you full access for up to 3 accounts — enough to run a real deal cycle and see exactly what Whyzer does for your pipeline. No credit card required. No time limit. If it helps you close deals, you'll know.
